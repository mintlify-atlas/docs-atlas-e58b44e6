## What is gSubs?

gSubs is a multi-platform, open-source desktop application built with Electron that finds the perfect subtitle match for your videos. Simply drag and drop your video files to get started - no more browsing through mysterious directories or searching on sketchy websites.

<Note>
  gSubs is completely free and open-source, licensed under MIT.
</Note>

- AVI (`.avi`)
- MKV (`.mkv`)
- MPG (`.mpg`)
- WebM (`.webm`)
- M4V (`.m4v`)

## How it works

When you drag and drop a video file, gSubs searches for subtitles using a two-tier approach:

1. **SubDB lookup**: First, gSubs checks SubDB using the video file's hash for an exact match
2. **OpenSubtitles search**: If no match is found, gSubs performs a deep search on OpenSubtitles using the filename and metadata

This dual-source strategy ensures you get the most accurate subtitle match possible.

<Tip>
  For best results, keep your video files named clearly with the movie or episode title.
</Tip>
